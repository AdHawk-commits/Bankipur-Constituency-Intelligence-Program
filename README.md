# Bankipur Assembly Constituency Intelligence Platform: Data-Driven Governance and Electoral Insights\

Self-assigned data-driven political consulting project analysing Bihar's constituencies using government datasets, electoral data, non-state actors survey reports and public policy indicators
---------------------------- 

## Problem Statement

Political decision-making is often constrained by fragmented data across governance, socio-economic indicators, public finance and electoral outcomes. This project integrates multiple public datasets to develop actionable insights for political consulting and evidence-based policymaking in Bihar.

---------------------------- 

## Objectives

- Analyse Bihar using district-level socio-economic and governance data.
- Identify regional development disparities.
- Build interactive dashboards and visualizations.
- Generate policy recommendations based on data.
- Develop a political consulting portfolio showcasing analytical skills.

---------------------------- 

## Scope

The project focuses on Bihar and combines data from government sources, non-state actors (CSOs, NGOs, think tanks, MNCs, etc.) survey reports and election-related datasets to analyse governance, development, demographics, and public service delivery.

---------------------------- 

## Expected Outcomes

- Clean, analysis-ready datasets
- Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
- Interactive Power BI Dashboard
- Python-based data analysis
- Policy Brief

- Presentation Deck

## Proposed Datasets

- Census 2011
- National Family Health Survey (NFHS-5)
- National Sample Survey (NSO)
- Bihar Open Data Portal for government development indicators
- Election Commission of India results (2005, 2010, 2015, and 2025)
- Booth-level Form-20 results
- ADR/MyNeta candidate affidavits
- Police station and crime statistics
- OpenStreetMap GiS Layers
- Local news and manifesto commitments during rallies
